A platform to implement and fund

public action in your area

Connect to your community

to Build Pablic Projects

WeServe makes it easy to find neardy people who are passionate about the same local issues that you are, so you can implemment Projects and change your communities together

MAKE A CHANGE

Start with an idea and get your

communities work on it

You want to take action and solve a public issue, but you need collaboration to implement your solution. Post your project idea, invite people to participate and lead the collaboration.
